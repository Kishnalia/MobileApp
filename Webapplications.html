BEST MOBILE APPLICATIONS

Social Media

Facebook

The Facebook app allows users to connect with friends, share photos and videos, post updates, join groups, and engage with various content.

Instagram

Instagram is a photo and video sharing platform where users can follow friends, celebrities, and brands, as well as discover and engage with visual content.

Business

Shopee

Shopee is a fast-growing online shopping marketplace that is known for everyday good prices. It is famous for deals and promotions.

TikTok

TikTok is a video-sharing app that allows users to create and share short-form videos on any topic.

Education

Google Classroom

Google Classroom is a free online service that lets teachers and students easily share files with each other

Microsoft Teams

Microsoft Teams is essentially a central point for groups, classes or friends to video call, chat, share and collaborate.
